! Mochi Recipes !

Dishes: Mochi is involved in many Asian desserts, both sweet and savory! Dango: Dango is a dish made of rice cakes wrapped in a red bean filling and skewered Chikara Udon: This noodle soup is commonly served with mochi cakes in it for an awesome combination of textures! Hawaiian Mango Mochi: This mochi dessert originates far away from Asia, I guess the entire world is full of mochi fans! This super sweet dessert is almost like cake. Mochi isn’t limited to the recipes you find either, try adding it as a side to your favorite meals!

Recipe: Mochi is usually made by steaming and pounding glutinous rice flour or mochigome rice until sticky.


    Full recipe:
  • 1 cup of rice flour (mochiko)(optional) 1 teaspoon of flavoring of choice, try matcha powder!
  • 1 cup water
  • ¼ cup of white sugar
  • ½ cup of cornstarch for rolling the mochi dough

Mix water, flour and sugar until smooth. Microwave for 3 mins and 30 seconds, or use a steamer if you have one. While the mochi is still hot, cover your surface with cornstarch and begin rolling the mochi into balls around 2 tsp. Store in paper muffin tin liners and serve!
